William Shakespeare - One of the greatest writers in the English language

  • William Shakespeare was an English poet, playwright and actor.
  • Most of his known works were produced between 1589 and 1613.
  • His early works were primarily comedies, histories and tragedies.
  • Some of his best works include Hamlet, Othello, King Lear and Macbeth.

Data source - Project Gutenburg

  • This project will visualize words used in his works in the form of a wordcloud.
  • Corpus of text used in this visualization are from Project Gutenburg.
  • Project Gutenburg provides over 49,000 free ebooks which can be downloaded.
